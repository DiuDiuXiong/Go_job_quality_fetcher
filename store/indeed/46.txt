We are a regulated digital asset firm looking for a talented Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the reliability, availability, and performance of our infrastructure and applications, and will play a critical role in maintaining the trust of our customers.




Responsibilities:

Work with the engineering team to design, build, and maintain our cloud infrastructure on AWS.
Build and manage Kubernetes clusters for our production and development environments.
Develop and maintain automation tools and scripts using Python and other languages to improve system reliability and reduce manual effort.
Design and engineer software release pipelines to enable rapid and reliable deployment of code changes.
Build and maintain monitoring and alerting systems to ensure the early detection and resolution of issues before they affect customers.
Help with level 2 support and incident response to ensure high availability and reliability of our systems.
Participate in on-call rotations to respond to critical issues outside of business hours.



Requirements:

Strong experience with AWS services and infrastructure, including EC2, S3, RDS, and VPC.
Proficiency with Kubernetes and container orchestration. Experience with scripting languages, particularly Python.
Experience with software release engineering, including CI/CD pipelines (in particular GitLab)
Understanding of networking principles and protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP, DNS, SSL).
Experience with monitoring and alerting systems, such as Prometheus or Grafana
Ability to work collaboratively with engineering and operations teams.
Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.



Preferred qualifications:

Experience in a regulated environment, ideally with financial services.
Familiarity with security best practices and regulatory compliance.
Knowledge of database technologies such as MySQL.
Experience with infrastructure-as-code tools such as Terraform.



If you are a talented and driven SRE with a passion for maintaining high availability and reliability in a fast-paced environment, we would love to hear from you. This is an excellent opportunity to join a leading, regulated digital asset firm and contribute to the success of a rapidly growing business.
