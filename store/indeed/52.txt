Job Title: Customer-Focused Systems Reliability Engineer (Fluent in Japanese) (REMOTE) Australia -

Job Description: We are seeking a versatile and customer-centric professional to join our dynamic Systems Reliability team. As a Customer-Focused Systems Reliability Engineer, you will have a unique opportunity to assist customers in deploying, maintaining, and troubleshooting our advanced distributed services and applications across both cloud and on-premise infrastructure. Your expertise and commitment to exceptional customer support will play a crucial role in ensuring customer satisfaction and success. If you are passionate about resolving complex issues and enjoy taking ownership of customer challenges, this role is perfect for you.

Responsibilities:

Embrace a customer-first approach, leveraging your technical expertise to identify, troubleshoot, and resolve product issues.
Provide top-notch technical support in Japanese to our valued customers based in Japan.
Demonstrate agility in delivering prompt and accurate updates, meeting SLAs, and providing timely resolutions for customer issues.
Contribute to the creation of comprehensive knowledge-base articles, enabling customers to self-serve and access valuable resources.
Deepen your understanding of data centers and public cloud environments (VMware, AWS, Azure, GCP) to effectively support customers' diverse requirements.
Maintain, monitor, and troubleshoot our cutting-edge cloud infrastructure.
Collaborate closely with Engineering teams to define and implement innovative tools and strategies that enhance debuggability, supportability, availability, scalability, and performance.
Cultivate expertise in cloud and on-premise infrastructure through the development of automation solutions and adherence to best practices.
Participate actively in the on-call rotation for critical SRE systems, lead comprehensive incident reviews, and conduct thorough root cause analyses.
Foster a comprehensive understanding of cloud NetOps and SecOps aspects, ensuring robust and secure infrastructure operations.

Requirements:

Fluent in both Japanese and English, enabling effective communication with our Japanese-speaking customers.
Possess a B.S. degree in Computer Science or relevant industry experience, showcasing your strong technical foundation.
Demonstrate proficiency in troubleshooting Linux Systems, underscoring your hands-on experience.
Exhibit familiarity with virtualization and Cloud technologies, enabling you to effectively navigate and support cloud-based environments.
Showcase a track record of excellence in enterprise customer support, including participation in on-call rotations for critical SRE systems and leading incident reviews and root cause analyses.
Proven ability to diagnose technical problems and collaborate closely with Engineering teams to resolve escalated issues.
Strong problem-solving skills, emphasizing algorithmic thinking and a holistic understanding of system architecture and behavior.
Possess a comprehensive understanding of tools and frameworks necessary to operate and manage Cloud infrastructure.
Showcase exceptional customer service skills, including the ability to empathize with customer challenges and provide effective solutions.
Strong communication skills and the ability to work autonomously, demonstrating self-motivation and proactive problem-solving abilities.
Proficiency in leveraging automation, monitoring, and data analysis to ensure high availability and optimal performance.
Familiarity with programming languages such as C/C++, Python, Go, or Java, demonstrating your versatility.
Exposure to infrastructure and service monitoring tools, allowing you to effectively monitor and optimize system performance.

If you are a customer-focused professional who thrives on tackling complex challenges and enjoys working with cutting-edge technologies, we invite you to join our team. Contribute to our customers' success while furthering your own career growth.
