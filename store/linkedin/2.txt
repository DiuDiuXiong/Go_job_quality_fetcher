Our purpose




Here at Datacom we connect people and technology in order to solve challenges, create opportunities and discover new possibilities for the communities we live in.




Our team




Working as a bridge between application technologies and customer support teams, Platform Management SRE’s work to take customer requirements and business information to support, design or re-design, develop, test and implement solutions. The Core Services Enterprise Tools group are committed to Agile delivery with a focus on Automation and a DevOps approach.




About The Role




In this role as Site Reliability Engineer, you will be providing technical support and expertise, testing new solutions, responding to feedback, and actively participating in future technology road mapping and implementation as well as maintaining and supporting those solutions as they roll throughout our business.




Australian Citizenship is required for this role due to the requirement to obtain a Baseline Security Clearance.




What You’ll Do




As Site Reliability Engineer, you will be responsible for, but not limited to, the following:




Administer and support the suite of tools at the platform layer
Ensure critical integrations and automations are developed and maintained for the platform toolsets
Maintaining, developing and continuously improving existing software
Providing technical support and consultancy as required
Actively participating in the evolution of the SRE function and capability




What You’ll Bring




What does success look like in this role? You will have a strong IT, business and/ or computer science background; as well as a solid base of technical skills. You will have excellent communication skills, proven ability in continual process improvement and the ability to manage stakeholder expectations. Prior experience with Microsoft, Unix / Linux, PHP, Monitoring & automation technologies and python scripting is important and you will ideally have experience working across a large, complex IT organisation, with a broad range of toolsets. Other qualities we are looking for include an understanding of Cloud and Cloud adoption. You will be highly adaptable and embrace change, you will promote diversity of thought and you know that making the odd mistake is ok as long as we learn from them. You will be used to dealing with competing priorities and pragmatic about decision making under those circumstances. You’ll be fun, enthusiastic and keen to develop both personally and for the organisation.




The Finer Details




We have over 6,500 people across our global offices and generate an annual revenue of over NZ $1.2 billion annually. This makes us one of Australasia’s largest professional IT services companies.




We have extensive expertise in operating data centres, IT services, software engineering and application management, as well as payroll and customer service design and operations.




With this comes a long list of significant clients and Datacom is committed to hiring, developing and promoting the best talent from a diverse range of backgrounds to serve those clients.




We are local at heart, yet world-class in capability.
