Sydney

Exciting opportunity to work closely with highly collaborative technology and business teams to drive ongoing system changes and enhancements.

As a Junior Devops Engineer, you will be part of the Commodities Global Markets Technology Platform team, where you will provide effective first and second level support for applications, both in-house and vendor supplied.

You will be part of the SRE team in Sydney; and collectively with other support team in London and Gurugram form a global "Follow the Sun" model.

As a member of the Commodities Global Markets Technology team, you will be working directly with the business as well as developers and global operational teams. You will be responsible for the support of critical applications used by the business for regulatory transaction reporting of Commodities and Financial products. This is a role that deals with time sensitive service deliverables in which accuracy of processing and timely escalation and communication is essential.

To be successful in this role you will have:

Experience within the financial industry focusing on Regulatory Reporting platforms
Take opportunities to identify, prioritize and address operational improvements, resolve user queries, eliminate manual processes, improve documentation, and fix defects.
Experience handling Incident and Problems to ensure healthy and stable environments.
Self-managing, proactive, able to work with minimal supervision.
Experience writing SQL queries and interacting with databases (Sybase/Oracle).
Good working knowledge of Unix
Knowledge of ITIL tools (e.g. Remedy) for Incident/Change/Problem management.
Experience and understanding of Agile development lifecycle and Scrum.
Experience with DevOps and Continuous Integration tools (e.g. Jira, Bitbucket, Bamboo).
