Melbourne, Sydney

We’re looking for a SRE director and leader that is passionate about reliability and software engineering. We’re seeking an individual that deeply understands software reliability, building at scale and driving strategic and tactical direction for reliability at our bank. This pivotal role is a huge opportunity to establish one of the best SRE teams in financial services.

This is a strategic leadership role, you will be responsible for overseeing the direction, execution and continuous improvement of our SRE initiatives. There will be early focus on building observability & reliability capabilities. Requiring strong influencing skills with development teams.

Define and execute the strategic direction for SRE initiatives
Establish Observability, SLO’s and error budgets, tools, practices and implementation.
Establish reliable software development and release engineering patterns
Implement SRE best practices, guidelines to enable reliability, velocity, performance and scale.
Responsible for strategically driving initiatives to improve MTTx metrics
Drive continuous improvement initiatives including adoption of automation, monitoring and incident management tools and practices
Lead, mentor, coach a team of SREs, software engineers and 24x7 operators
Foster a culture of collaboration, learning, and knowledge sharing.
Strong prioritisation skills and driving OKR’s
Leading and influencing with data insights
Strong software engineering and release engineering experience

You will have experience in a similar leadership role, having been exposed to multiple different technologies and be passionate about technology. Your role will require you to engage senior business representatives to understand their motivations, using your knowledge of emerging technologies to help shape the combined business and technology strategies

What’s in it for you:

This is an opportunity to play a pivotal role in shaping Site Reliability Engineering for Macquarie Bank.
Opportunity to build SRE from the ground up and make a real impact on how SRE is implemented in the financial services industry.
Learn from the best engineers in Australia and work on market leading products
Be part of a team that deeply values diversity and creates space for you to be your best
Being hands-on with the latest cloud technologies including Google Cloud to tackle interesting banking and finance problems
Our work environment is modern and inclusive with a strong focus on employee experience
Flexible work options, including working from home
Ongoing professional development and free technical certification
Highly competitive remuneration and bonuses
