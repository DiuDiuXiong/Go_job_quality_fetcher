Further develop your commercial capability and acumen and join the world of consulting! At JDS, we’re a collaborative, technically curious team who love doing challenging work that solves meaningful client problems. We’ve got a proud history of implementing, managing and supporting industry-leading IT solutions and we’re looking to grow our 100+ team across Australia.




Our team is vendor-aligned and possesses in-depth specialist knowledge and proven skills to ensure we continue to deliver excellence to our clients and nurture an environment of continued growth and development for our people.




As part of our exciting growth plans, we have an opportunity for a Technical Consultant to join our New Relic Practice in Sydney. 

Your role:

Work closely with clients to understand their business objectives and technical requirements, offering guidance and recommendations for implementing SRE practices.
Leverage your knowledge of New Relic to design and implement comprehensive monitoring solutions that cover the entire technology stack, enabling proactive issue detection and performance optimisation.
Enhance system reliability by implementing strategies and tools that ensure high uptime performance, minimising downtime and maximising user satisfaction.
Analyse system performance metrics, identify bottlenecks and areas for improvement, and provide recommendations for performance tuning to enhance efficiency and scalability.
Provide training, support, and documentation to clients, enabling them to effectively utilise SRE platforms, interpret monitoring data, and leverage advanced features for system optimisation.

A little about you: 

Experience in consulting roles specialising in Site Reliability Engineering platforms, with an understanding of SRE principles, methodologies, and best practices.
Knowledge of techniques to ensure high uptime performance, including fault tolerance, load balancing, and failover mechanisms.
Ability to analyse system performance metrics, diagnosing performance bottlenecks, and implementing tuning strategies to optimise system efficiency and scalability.
Familiarity with integrating AI Ops capabilities into monitoring platforms, leveraging machine learning and automation for proactive incident management and resolution.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to effectively collaborate with clients, understand their requirements, and provide clear and concise technical guidance.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills to identify and resolve technical issues in a timely and efficient manner.




Why JDS? 

Receive ongoing investment into your career through our training/certification pathways.
A flexible approach to work - with a positive and down to Earth culture. 
An environment that is inclusive and promotes and supports diversity in the workplace, where you are supported and encouraged to be your whole self.
