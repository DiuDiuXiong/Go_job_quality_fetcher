Melbourne, Sydney

We're seeking a Site Reliability Engineer with a passion for software reliability. We aim to provide the most reliable products and service in financial industry.

In this role you will be responsible for:

Application of Observability principles within our infrastructure, environments and software
Application of best practice SLI’s, SLO’s, metrics, and error budgets
Develop and implement comprehensive monitoring, logging and alerting technologies
Establish and implement strategies for canary releases, feature toggling and rollback back practices
Collaboration with feature teams to ensure reliability practices are integrated into development
Implement continuous learning through blameless postmortems
Participate in improving incident management and application of best practices.
Establishing Release Engineering best practices
Developing reliability software patterns and practices (e.g. circuit breaker, retry etc).
Help achieve the goal of ‘push on green’ for deployments

Skills required:

Strong experience in software engineering
Strong experience in SLO, metrics, logging, and tracing
Proven record of accomplishment in automating toil
Excellent understanding of modern software development practices, tools and technologies
A passion for getting things done, balancing short- and long-term needs
Influencing software engineers on reliability
Influence and technical leadership capabilities to drive change in environments, manage stakeholders and get alignment cross functionally
Strong DevOps fundamentals with preference for Java, Golang, Microservices and other cloud technologies

What is in it for you:

This is an opportunity to play a pivotal role in shaping Engineering for Macquarie Bank
Enjoy the satisfaction of making a difference and being part of a team that drives positive change in the bank and the industry
Be part of a forward-thinking organisation that embraces new technologies and modern engineering practices
Be part of a team that deeply values diversity and creates space for you to be your best, in a modern & inclusive environment with a strong focus on employee experience
Being hands-on with the latest cloud technologies including Google Cloud to tackle interesting banking and finance problems
Ongoing professional development and free technical certification
