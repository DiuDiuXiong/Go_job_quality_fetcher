About The Role

The company is looking for a highly reliable and autonomous DevOps Engineer who will be involved in many different phases of the software application lifecycle including Web Apps and Web Services, Games as well as blockchains & NFT's in the near future.

Responsibilities

Work in a fast-paced DevSecOps environment and deliver our products to market quickly and efficiently
Implement and maintain CI/CD pipeline for deployment and test automation
Monitor application health and performance and be proactive in anticipating production issues such as outages, slowness, processing delays, errors, and failures, and take corrective action to prevent them
Provide continued improvement to the platform infrastructure through automation and standardization

Requirements

Hands-on experience with AWS services (Lambda, ECS, EC2, ALB, ASGs, CW, RDS, SNS, VPC, SG, IAM, SQL/NoSQL, etc but you don't need to know all!)
Hands-on experience with Infra as Code (CloudFormation, Pulumi, CDK, or Terraform)
Hands-on experience with Docker
Experience with monitoring and reliability (this role is more SRE than Sys engineering)
Ability to build complete CI/CD workflows - GitLab is preferred
Familiarity with GitOps and DevSecOps
Basic knowledge in any programming languages: Shell, Bash, Typescript, Go, Python, etc
Basic knowledge of popular public & enterprise blockchains, cryptocurrency, smart contracts (Solidity), and NFTs.
