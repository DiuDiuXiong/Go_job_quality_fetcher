Kubernetes Engineer (On Premise) - Market rate salary - Hybrid Working 

Our client is seeking a highly skilled Kubernetes Platform Engineer to join their dynamic team. As a Kubernetes Platform Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, deploying, and maintaining the latest Kubernetes platform technologies to ensure optimal performance and scalability. The current platform is on prem with a plan to migrate to a mutli-cloud platform.

Offices in Brisbane/Sydney and they work very flexibly, preference is 2-3 days per week in the office.


The role

Support Application Development and Infrastructure teams
Maintaining and developing the Kubernetes platforms. 
Contributing to comprehensive Operations.
Assisting in remediating risks and writing policy as per Cybersecurity and Compliance guidance.
The promotion of continuous improvement.
Arranging outage windows with technical services and business contacts as required.
Participating in after-hours maintenance and support activities as required.
Experience

Containerisation solutions (On-prem Kubernetes, Docker, VMware TKG& associated products)
Containerisation Security principles
Assisting with developing and troubleshooting CI/CD Pipelines. Eg. Azure DevOps(On-premise), Tanzu Application Platform
VMware Virtualisation (VMware vSphere, NSX-T& associated products)
Basic/Intermediate Linux Administration 
Intermediate networking concepts 
Splunk Monitoring
